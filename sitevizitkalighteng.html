About us

The association of Narva artists "Vestervalley" was founded in 1999 by art critic Karin Taidre. "Westervalley" brings together 37 artists working in different techniques and directions. The association includes painters, graphic artists, artists of applied art, designers and photographers of Narva. The main purpose of the association is to create joint exhibitions, events, as well as to improve the artistic qualifications of participants.

Many of the artists are or have been teaching at the Narva Art School, studios, as well as at the Narva College of the University of Tartu and the gymnasiums of Narva and Tallinn.

With its work, Vestervalley tries to promote Narva art both in Estonia and abroad. (Tallinn, Pärnu, Paide, Tartu, Purtse, Türi, Viljandi, Jõhvi, Kohtla-Järve, etc.), Russia (St. Petersburg, Yaroslavl, Kostroma, Petrozavodsk), Lithuania (Klaipeda), USA (Bel-Air, Maryland).

Every year, Westervalley participates in the Artist's Day, which takes place in early June, where residents and guests of the city can see exhibitions, promotions, participate in master classes, communicate with artists, etc.

The group is constantly replenished with young and new artists who are actively involved in the activities of the association.

Since 2017, the chairman of the board of the association is Olga Tyurina.
